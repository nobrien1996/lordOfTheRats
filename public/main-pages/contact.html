Contact Me



Ask me about brand or cosplay collaborations, commissions, or more!


Items available for commission include various styles of tunic, pants, dresses, arming wear, caps, hoods, and other fabric pieces from Antiquity and the Middle Ages. Inquire about any items not listed and we can see what's doable.

All items for commission are custom-made to order. Prices depend on cost of materials, complexity of item, and labor required. Prices do not include shipping costs.


For brand collaborations, feel free to reach out to me with any ideas that you may have or products that you're interested in having me advertised.


For any cosplay collaborations, feel free to reach out with any ideas you may have, as well as where you're located, and we can see what we can figure out to do!
